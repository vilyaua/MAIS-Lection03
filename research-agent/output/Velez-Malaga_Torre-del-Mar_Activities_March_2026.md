# Activities and Events in Velez-Malaga and Torre del Mar for March 2026

## Overview
This report provides information about available activities and events in Velez-Malaga and Torre del Mar, Spain, during March 2026. Both towns have rich cultural calendars with a strong emphasis on traditional festivals, cultural events, and community celebrations. Easter 2026, falling at the end of March, is a focal point for several festivities.

## Velez-Malaga Activities and Events in March 2026

### Ferias and Fiestas
- **Feria de Cajiz**: Typically held over a weekend in mid-March (around March 16-18). This is a local fair with cultural and recreational activities.

### Easter / Semana Santa (Holy Week)
- Easter in 2026 is on March 29.
- Semana Santa festivities take place during the week leading up to Easter Sunday (March 23-29).
- These include elaborate religious processions, community events, and cultural celebrations.

### Other Events and Cultural Activities
- Velez-Malaga hosts various music concerts, open-air theater performances, and outdoor cinema sessions seasonally.
- Other annual events include flamenco fusion concerts, live jazz, and gastronomic festivals.

### Sports
- A notable event is the XIII Media Maratón Vélez-Málaga 2026 scheduled for May, indicating regular sporting events in the region.

## Torre del Mar Activities and Events in March 2026

### Religious and Cultural Events
- Semana Santa is a major event with widespread participation.
- Torre del Mar observes various religious festivals, with strong community involvement.

### Regular Festivals
- Most prominent festivals like Fiesta del Carmen occur in July, not in March.

## Nearby Activities in the Malaga Region in March 2026
- Several events happen in nearby towns and Malaga city, including:
  - Classic Car Rally in Fuengirola (March 22).
  - International Choral Festival in Antequera (March 12-16).
  - Magic shows, concerts, and artisan markets at Benalmádena marina throughout March.
  - Theatre performances and operas in Estepona.
  - Harley Davidson Rally in Benalmádena (March 20-22).
  - Gastronomic festivals, street markets, and wine tastings.

## Conclusion
Visitors to Velez-Malaga and Torre del Mar in March 2026 can expect to enjoy traditional Spanish cultural experiences, particularly linked to the Easter celebrations. While specific local fairs and community events take place, the broader Malaga region offers a vibrant array of music, festivals, and outdoor activities during this period.
